<h1>Introduction<h1>
This Problem contains details of uber rides of a customer.

<br>
<h2>Dataset<h2>
The dataset contains Start Date, End Date, Start Location, End Location, Miles Driven and Purpose of drive (Business, Personal, Meals etc)

<br>
<h2>Objective<h2>
To fetch insights from the behavior of an common Uber customer.
<br>

<br>
<h2>Conclusion<h2>
<ol>User mainly uses Uber cabs for its Business purposes
<ol>Around 94% miles was consumed during Business trips.
<ol>Only 6% miles were consumed during personal trips.
<ol>There are 177 unique starting points
<ol>Cary is most poplular starting point for this driver.
<ol>There are 188 unique Stop points.
<ol>Cary is most poplular drop point for this driver.
<ol>Cary-Durham & Cary-Morrisville and vice versa are the User's longest distance Uber ride.
<ol>User usually takes single-trip Uber rides.
<ol>Around 75% trip is single-trip and 25% are Round-Trip.
<ol>User's Most number of rides were in month of December & Least were in September.
<ol>Friday has maximum number of trips.
<ol>Afternoons and evenings seem to have the maximum number of trips.
<ol>User's Longest ride were on April & shortest were on November
